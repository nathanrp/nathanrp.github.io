TCS

TCS is a giant whale of a company. According to their website, TCS employs 509,058 employees, which represent 155 nationalities across 46 countries. So having an impact on a product and organization that large was very difficult. I would like to say I practiced UX design there, but in reality I was doing a lot of UI, interaction, and visual design. It was a great learning experience. I learned how to use design tools to speed up my work. I saw a lot of the sales process. I worked in the financial services division and worked on products for banking, insurance, capital markets, corporate governance, and more.
