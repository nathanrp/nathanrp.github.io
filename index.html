About me

With 20+ years of experience as a designer, I’ve worked in Fintech, SaaS, Education, Security, B2B, and other industries and organizations, including Housecall Pro, CircleCI, Mavenlink (now Kantata), H&B Block, TCS, WGU, and The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Throughout my career, I’ve taken on roles ranging from individual contributor to lead and management, driving projects from concept to completion (0–1). I enjoy human-centered design and working with others to create better products and experiences that are good for people and the business, resulting in better engagement and retention.
