Onboarding Experience

Pros struggle to know where to start, how to navigate, and effectively use the many features in HCP, leading to underutilization of the product.

Desired outcome

A better onboarding experience that helps pros quickly discover the most valuable aspects of the product. This approach was designed to drive higher engagement and ultimately improve customer retention.

Our Solution

We created an onboarding strategy that could be shared across product teams. This strategy includes a set of principles, design patterns, and reusable components that teams could implement to improve the engagement in their specific areas of the product.
