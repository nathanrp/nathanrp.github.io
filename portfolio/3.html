About TCS

TCS is a giant company. According to their website, TCS employs 509,058 employees, which represent 155 nationalities across 46 countries. So having an impact on a product and organization that large was very difficult.

Roles & Responsibilities

  1. UI & UX Designer
  2. I worked in the financial services division and worked on products for banking, insurance, capital markets, corporate governance, and more.
  3. I learned how to use design tools to speed up my work. I saw a lot of the sales process and how enterprise software is sold.
  4. I facilitated a lot of design thinking workshops with clients and guided them through design decisions and our design process.
  5. I worked with off-shore product teams and engineering teams and worked on projects that cost millions of dollars and spanned multiple years.
