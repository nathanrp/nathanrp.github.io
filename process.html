Customers, collaboration, confidence. My take on the design process.

My design process is pretty simple. Talk to customers, a lot. Collaborate with your team: product managers, engineers, researchers, writers, SMEs. Prototype until you are confident in your solution. Then release it into the wild and see what happens. Everything else is… it depends.
